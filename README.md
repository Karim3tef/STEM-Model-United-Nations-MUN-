# STEM Model United Nations (STEM MUN)
Welcome to the official repository for STEM Model United Nations (STEM MUN), a dynamic academic simulation where participants step into the roles of global diplomats to debate and resolve pressing international issues using real-world UN procedures.

# About STEM MUN
STEM MUN offers a prestigious platform for youth, particularly those with a passion for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, to:

- Debate international agendas in a parliamentary setting.
- Develop resolutions to address global challenges.
- Enhance key skills such as public speaking, writing, critical thinking, and teamwork.
- By fostering an atmosphere of diplomacy, collaboration, and innovation, STEM MUN empowers young leaders to share diverse perspectives and broaden their understanding of global policies.

# Key Features
- Realistic UN Simulations: Participants engage in structured debates following official UN protocols.
- Skill Development: Boosts communication, problem-solving, and negotiation skills.
- STEM-Centric Themes: Focus on STEM-related global issues, inspiring innovative solutions.
- Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded peers from diverse backgrounds.

# Mission
STEM MUN aims to create a space where young STEM enthusiasts can merge their passion for technology and science with an understanding of international relations, preparing them to become future leaders and change-makers.
